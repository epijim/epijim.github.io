3D scatter plot in R

Get the base map from google

Just need x, y and z in that order.

library(rgl)

attach(plotdata)
	plot3d(latitude, longitude, altitude, col="red", size=1)
detach(plotdata)

On a mac, an XQuartz window will open up with the plot.
