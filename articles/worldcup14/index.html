Changing fate of the German football team

As the final game of the World Cup started, Germany had gone from outsiders to the team expected to win, while Brazil had fallen in disgrace from firm favourites to 4th place.

FiveThirtyEight released updated predictions on who would win the World Cup at the end of each game. When I saw they had the raw data of their game by game predictions, I figured it would be cool to plot the changes in each teams predicted chance of winning over time.

During the competition;

  • Germany peaked with a 64% chance of winning on the day of the final
  • Brazil peaked with a 56% chance of winning, in the days leading up to their 7:1 loss to Germany.
