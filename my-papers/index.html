My papers

Below is a list of my first authored papers, followed by papers I co-authored. Clicking on one of my first authored papers will take you to another page that briefly summarises what the paper was about.

First authored papers

  • 1. Modelling the effect of intensive treatment on longterm cardiovascular disease risk

    Taking a trial of intensive treatment for diabetes, I predicted the difference in long-term outcomes using cardio-metobolic health at the last available follow up point.

  • 2. Cardiovascular disease risk factors after diabetes diagnosis

    Looked at how risk factors for cardiovascular disease change after diabetes diagnosis in a population with screen-detected diabetes.

  • 3. Bariatric surgery in children

    Completed a meta-analysis looking at the effectiveness of bariatric surgery in children and adolescents.

  • 4. Recovery after injury

    Completed a meta-analysis of EQ-5D assessed recovery after injury combining three thirds of the known study data.
