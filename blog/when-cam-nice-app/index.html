When is it nice in Cambridge (interactive app)?

Filed under: r, weather, cambridge, app

Last week I plotted when the weather is nice in Cambridge.

After getting pointed towards the University Weather Station data, I decided to recreate it using the this better data source as an interactive app where people can define what nice means themselves.

This app is built using R, and hosted on the free Shiny servers on ShinyApps.io.
